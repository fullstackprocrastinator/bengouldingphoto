A Little About Me

What started as a passion for the speed and drama of motorcycle racing has evolved into a broader love for capturing peak moments in sport and life. My goal is always the same: to freeze a moment in time that tells a story of dedication, emotion, and action.

Now, I'm channeling that same energy into Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu and other sports, finding the art within the athleticism. Whether it's on the track, the mat, or a simple portrait session, I bring a unique perspective to create powerful and lasting images.
